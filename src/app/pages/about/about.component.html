About TMI

TMI (Threat Modeling Improved) is a collaborative platform for creating, managing, and sharing threat models. Our mission is to make security threat modeling accessible, efficient, and integrated into the software development lifecycle.

The platform features interactive diagram creation, real-time collaboration, and comprehensive threat documentation capabilities. Built with modern web technologies, TMI helps teams identify, analyze, and mitigate security threats through collaborative modeling.

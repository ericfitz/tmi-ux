The Operator is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is handled.

Information Collection and Use

When users authenticate via social login (using OAuth or OpenID Connect providers such as Sign in with Google, Login with GitHub, Microsoft Entra External ID, or others), the application collects the following information from the selected provider, solely to facilitate authentication and provide personalized services:

  • Basic Profile Information: Full name, email address, and profile picture (if provided and available).
  • Unique Identifier: A provider-specific user ID to manage account sessions securely.

No additional personal data is collected without explicit user consent. We do not access or store sensitive information such as passwords, contacts, or other provider-specific data beyond what is necessary for authentication. All collected information is handled in accordance with the respective provider's API policies and our commitment to user privacy.

Data Sharing

We do not share your personal information with any third parties, except in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: Your data may be stored and processed on secure third-party cloud infrastructure (such as AWS, Google Cloud, or similar providers) solely to operate and maintain the application. These providers are contractually obligated to protect your data and use it only for the purposes we specify.
  • Legal Compliance: We may disclose information if required by law, such as in response to a valid court order, subpoena, or government request.

Beyond these exceptions, we do not sell, rent, or share your data with any other entities for marketing, advertising, or any other purpose.

Data Security

The Operator implements industry-standard security measures to protect your personal data, including encryption in transit and at rest, secure authentication protocols, and regular security updates. Access to your information is restricted to authorized personnel on a need-to-know basis.

While no system is entirely impervious to risks, The Operator continuously monitors and updates safeguards to mitigate threats and ensure the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of your data.

User Rights

You have full control over your personal data. At any time, you may access the "Delete All My Data" feature in the User Preferences section of the application.

This option enables immediate deletion of all data The Operator stores on your behalf, including profile information and authentication records. Upon activation, your data will be permanently removed from The Operator's systems and third-party providers, subject to any legal retention obligations.

Legal Compliance

The Operator will only disclose information if required to do so by law, such as in response to a valid government order or subpoena. In such cases, The Operator will comply with applicable legal obligations.

Changes to This Policy

The Operator may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.
